Step 1

Design your ad

Open the Design tab to set your headline, body copy, CTA, and brand name. Choose from 6 template styles (Standard, Before/After, Testimonial, Stats, Spotlight, Bold Statement) and customise colours, fonts, and layout.

  • Character counters warn you about Meta limits (40 headline, 125 body)
  • Pick from 4 aspect ratios: 1:1, 4:5, 9:16, 16:9
  • Upload a background image or use gradient colours
  • Save colour presets as reusable schemes
  • Toggle grain texture and accent glow effects

Step 2

Research your audience

The Research tab lets you search Reddit for real conversations about your product space. Paste your marketing brief and the AI extracts search keywords automatically.

  • Paste a marketing brief to auto-extract Reddit search terms
  • Search by keyword, subreddit, and sort (relevance / top / new)
  • Save quotes from real user discussions
  • Click "Send to Brainstormer" to pass quotes directly to AI generation

Step 3

Brainstorm copy with AI

The Brainstorm tab generates ad copy variations using either built-in templates or Claude AI. Each variation gets a headline effectiveness score so you know what's strong before you export.

  • Templates mode: instant generation from product details + tone
  • AI Generate mode: paste a brief or upload a PDF/TXT file
  • 5 tones: Professional, Playful, Urgent, Luxury, Minimal
  • Liked variations influence future AI output
  • Undo button to remove just-added variations

Step 4

Preview & compare

The live preview updates instantly as you edit. Click any variation thumbnail to see it on the canvas. Like your favourites, then open Compare mode to see them side by side.

  • Canvas view shows the full ad creative
  • Feed Preview shows how it looks in a Meta feed
  • "Copy for Meta" copies headline + body + CTA to clipboard
  • Like 2+ variations to unlock side-by-side comparison
  • Compare up to 4 variations simultaneously

Save & load projects

Click Project in the header to save your entire workspace as a JSON file â€” or load a previously saved project. This preserves your design config, all variations, and liked IDs.

  • Save Project downloads a .json file with all your work
  • Load Project restores config, variations, and liked IDs
  • Confirmation dialog prevents accidental overwrites
  • Share project files with teammates

Step 7

Publish to Meta

Go straight from design to live ads. Publish to Meta sends a single creative, or use Bulk Publish to push all your variations at once. Track performance in the Performance tab.

  • Single publish with headline, body, CTA, and image
  • Bulk publish all variations in one action
  • Performance tab shows spend, clicks, CTR, CPC
  • "Remix Winner" sends top performers back to Brainstorm
